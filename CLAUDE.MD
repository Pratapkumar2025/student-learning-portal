# Student Learning Portal - Project Context

## Project Overview

This is an educational web portal designed for students to access learning resources, current affairs, and career opportunities. The portal serves as a comprehensive learning platform with multiple features to support student education.

## Purpose

To provide students with a centralized platform for:
- Staying updated with daily news and current affairs
- Testing knowledge through quizzes
- Accessing study materials across multiple subjects
- Learning about historical events
- Tracking upcoming exams
- Finding government job opportunities
- Enjoying motivational content

## Key Features

1. **All India Radio Live** - 24x7 news stream for current affairs
2. **Daily GK Quiz** - Interactive general knowledge quizzes
3. **Study Subjects** - Educational content for:
   - Science
   - Mathematics
   - Social Science
   - Hindi
4. **Today in History** - Historical events and facts
5. **Upcoming Exams** - Calendar of important examinations
6. **Job Recruitments** - Latest government job notifications
7. **Spotify Integration** - Motivational Hindi songs and social issues podcasts

## Development Guidelines

When working on this project:

1. **User Focus**: Keep students and teachers as the primary audience
2. **Accessibility**: Ensure content is accessible and easy to navigate
3. **Educational Value**: Prioritize educational content and learning outcomes
4. **Performance**: Keep the site lightweight and fast-loading
5. **Mobile-First**: Ensure responsive design for mobile devices
6. **Content Updates**: Regular updates to exam schedules and job listings
